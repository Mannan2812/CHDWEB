Testimonials

“ The broad roads adjoining the service roads keeps the traffic flow well in control. The avenue plantation gives this city the Green look as we drive on these roads. The Rose Garden with its wide varieties of roses offers a great place to walk around, is a treat to the eyes and mind. The Lake offers a great jogging track be it early morning or the evening. The Lake located in the heart of the city is always busy with local and outside population I love this city and always look forward to going back again. Being at the foot of the rocky mountains which lead to Shimla, it is the gateway to a great vacation. ”

— Mannan Bansal, PEC STUDENT

“This is one of the most beautiful and cleanest city of India. There are many place to visit like Rock Garden, Sukhna lake, Rose Garden and many other. And Saturday night at Sector 17 is awesome. And if you get hungry there lots of options available of every budget. Like there r several eating joints around sector 22, many restaurant in sector 35 where you can go. And travelling around is also very cheap and convenient in local CTU buses.”

— Munish Kumar Garg, PEC STUDENT

“I was in Chandigarh for a weekend and wanted an architectural tour. Chandigarh walkers accommodated my last minute request very well. Sapna tailored my tour to my interests, and was very informative. I had a great time visiting the architectural museum and le corbusier center with her, and she took me through parts of leisure valley, to the open hand, and the rock garden. We couldn't see much of the capitol complex because it was the weekend, but I knew that going in. I appreciated having such a well informed guide to Chandigarh's unique heritage. I also appreciated that Sapna was so willing and even happy to do this walking tour with me in the heat of mid-day in the summer!”

— Prathu Singal, PEC STUDENT
